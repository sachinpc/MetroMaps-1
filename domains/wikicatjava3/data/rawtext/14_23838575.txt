adminer adminer formerly known as phpminadmin is a tool for managing content in mysql databases since version  also in postgresql ms sql sqlite and oracle databases adminer is distributed under apache license or gpl v in a form of a single php file around  kib in size its author is jakub vrna who started to develop this tool as a lightweight alternative to phpmyadmin in july  adminer got some attention in  when it made it to the cca finals at sourceforge also first webhosting providers started to include adminer as mysql managing tool into their portfolio of services in  adminer got coverage on linuxcom for the second time the projects priorities according to its author are in this order safety userfriendliness performance functionality and size the small single file is a result of compilation and minification of source codes
